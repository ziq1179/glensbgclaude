Admissions

A clear, straightforward pathway to joining The Glens — with full transparency on fees, process, and what to expect at every step.

How It Works

The Admission Process

We have designed our admissions pathway to be as clear and supportive as possible. There is no pressure at any stage — we are here to help you make the right decision for your loved one.

1

Initial Enquiry

Call or email our team to discuss your loved one's needs and ask any initial questions. We are happy to talk through all options with you.

2

Visit & Assessment

Arrange a visit to The Glens to meet our team and see the home. A pre-admission assessment is completed within 24 hours of the enquiry being taken forward.

3

Care Plan & Agreement

We develop a personalised care plan together with the resident and their family. Fees, terms, and the one-month trial period are explained in full.

4

Moving In

Residents are encouraged to bring their own furniture and belongings to personalise their room. Our team supports a warm, comfortable transition into The Glens.

24-Hour Assessment Commitment

Once an enquiry is progressed, we commit to completing a full pre-admission assessment within 24 hours. We understand that families often need to act quickly, and we will always do our best to respond promptly and sensitively.

Making It Home

Your Room, Your Space

One of the most important things we offer at The Glens is genuine personalisation. We firmly believe that a care home room should feel like your room — not a generic space in an institution.

Residents are actively encouraged to bring their own furniture, pictures, ornaments, and personal items. If a favourite armchair makes a resident feel at home, we want it here. If a family photograph brings comfort, we will help hang it on the wall.

We mark personal milestones — birthdays, anniversaries, and other significant moments — with care and celebration. Residents are proud of their rooms, and we are proud to support that sense of ownership and identity.

  • Bring your own furniture, pictures, and cherished belongings
  • Single and en-suite room options available
  • Milestones and birthdays celebrated with warmth and care

Terms & Conditions

Trial Period & Occupancy Terms

We want every resident to feel confident and settled. Our one-month trial period is designed to ensure The Glens is the right home — with no long-term commitment required from day one.

One-Month Trial Period

All new residents are admitted on a one-month trial basis. This gives both the resident and the home the opportunity to ensure The Glens is the right fit, without pressure or long-term obligation from the outset.

Notice to Terminate

Either party may terminate the occupancy agreement during or after the trial period by providing one week's formal written notice. We believe in fair, transparent terms that respect the interests of both residents and their families.

Vacant Room Policy

In the event of permanent vacation without the required notice, a charge not exceeding one week's fee may apply. Full details are set out in the individual Terms of Business provided at admission.
