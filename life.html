Enrichment Programme

The Enrichment & Activity Programme

At The Glens, we actively challenge disengagement theory — the idea that older adults inevitably withdraw from life and activity. Our Enrichment and Activity Programme, led by our dedicated Activities Coordinator, ensures every resident has regular, meaningful opportunities to participate, connect, and enjoy.

We believe activities should be diverse, responsive to individual preferences, and genuinely fun. From gentle physical exercise to live entertainment, creative sessions to animal therapy, there is always something happening at The Glens.

Family members are always welcome to join in with activities and celebrations. We see the involvement of loved ones as enriching for everyone — residents and families alike.

  • Activities planned around individual interests and abilities
  • Both group sessions and one-to-one engagement offered
  • Regular review of the programme based on resident feedback
  • Families invited to join special events and celebrations

A snapshot of the activities, events, and moments that make life at The Glens so much more than care alone.

Yoga & Balloon Exercises

Gentle seated yoga and balloon exercises promote flexibility, coordination, and wellbeing — adapted to suit all abilities and mobility levels.

We host visiting musicians and entertainers regularly, bringing live performance into the home. These sessions are a highlight of the social calendar for many residents.

The Glens is home to resident chickens and a rabbit, providing daily opportunities for pet interaction — proven to reduce anxiety, improve mood, and provide gentle stimulation.

Every birthday is a celebration at The Glens. Milestone birthdays are marked with particular care and joy — residents are always encouraged to personalise how they celebrate.

Daily Dining

The Daily Menu — Fresh, Home-Cooked, Nutritious

Great food is central to the quality of life at The Glens. Our cook prepares fresh, varied, home-cooked meals every day, with menus designed to be both nutritionally balanced and genuinely enjoyable.

Our digital menu board mirrors the physical whiteboard displayed in the dining room each day — keeping residents, families, and staff informed of what's being served. Meals are captured and shared by our cook as part of our "Life at The Glens" transparency commitment.

We accommodate dietary requirements, religious preferences, and personal tastes. Good nutrition is not a nicety here — it is a cornerstone of quality care.

  • Fresh, home-cooked meals prepared daily on-site
  • Varied menus with seasonal produce where possible
  • All dietary, cultural, and personal preferences accommodated
  • Nutritional standards reviewed to support resident health

Family Transparency

Keeping Families Connected & Informed

We are committed to keeping families informed and involved in the life of their loved ones at The Glens — through photos, updates, and open communication.

Approved Photo Sharing

Our Activities Coordinator captures daily life — events, activities, and celebrations — through a secure staff portal. All images are reviewed by the Registered Manager before publication, ensuring privacy and dignity standards are always upheld.

Privacy & Dignity First

All media is reviewed against our Privacy and Dignity standards. Residents must be appropriately dressed and have provided documented consent before any image is published. Resident privacy is non-negotiable.

Open Communication

Our management team is always accessible to families. We actively encourage questions, feedback, and involvement — because the best care is delivered in genuine partnership with the people who love our residents.

Our Photo Workflow

Stage 1: Capture — the Activities Coordinator or Cook photographs meals and events via secure mobile portal → Stage 2: Approval — Miss Sheena Magee (Registered Manager) audits all media for Privacy & Dignity compliance and consent → Stage 3: Optimisation — images are compressed and alt-text descriptions added before publication.
