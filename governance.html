Regulatory Framework

Our Regulatory & Governance Hub

The Glens is committed to full transparency in all regulatory matters. The information below provides direct access to our regulatory bodies, inspection reports, and independent oversight organisations.

The Glens is registered with and regularly inspected by the Regulation and Quality Improvement Authority (RQIA) — the independent body responsible for monitoring health and social care services in Northern Ireland.

Complaints

Our Complaints Procedure

We take all feedback seriously — including complaints — because it helps us improve. If you have a concern about any aspect of care or service at The Glens, we want to hear from you and we will respond promptly and fairly.

All residents and their families have the right to raise a complaint without fear of recrimination. Our complaints procedure is designed to be accessible, transparent, and genuinely effective.

How to Raise a Concern

  • Step 1 — Speak to us directly. In the first instance, raise your concern with the Registered Manager, Miss Sheena Magee, or the Deputy Manager, Mrs. Finvola Sloane. Most concerns can be resolved quickly at this stage.
  • Step 2 — Put it in writing. If you would prefer to make a formal written complaint, please address it to the Registered Manager. You will receive an acknowledgement within 2 working days and a full response within 28 days.
  • Step 3 — Escalate to RQIA. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the RQIA directly. Their contact details are provided above.
  • Step 4 — Ombudsman. As a final step, the NI Public Services Ombudsman offers independent review of unresolved complaints.

Data & Privacy

Privacy Notice

The Glens Residential Home is committed to protecting the privacy and dignity of all residents, their families, and enquirers. We collect and process personal data only where necessary to deliver care services, respond to enquiries, and fulfil our regulatory obligations.

Personal data is stored securely, accessed only by authorised staff, and never shared with third parties except where required by law or regulatory authority. Residents' photographic data and media is subject to specific consent and privacy audit processes described in our Life at The Glens section.

For a full copy of our Privacy Policy, please contact Miss Sheena Magee (Registered Manager) at info@theglensresidentialhome.co.uk or by telephone on 028 2177 1234.

Legal

Terms of Business

The Terms of Business governing occupancy at The Glens Residential Home are provided in full to all residents and their representatives prior to admission. Key provisions include the one-month trial period, one-week written notice requirement for termination, the vacant room charge policy, and the annual April fee review process.

A copy of the current Terms of Business is available on request from the Registered Manager. Terms are reviewed annually each April to ensure compliance with current regulatory requirements and Trust funding agreements.

Annual Review Reminder

The Terms of Business and Fee Schedule are reviewed each April, coinciding with Northern Health and Social Care Trust payment increases. All residents and representatives are notified in writing of any changes in advance.
