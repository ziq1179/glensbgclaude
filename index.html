Our Commitment

Why Families Choose The Glens

Every decision we make is guided by our founding principle: "Homes are for living." Dignity, choice, and fulfilment are at the heart of everything we do.

A True Home Environment

Our single-storey bungalow design is built for comfort and accessibility. Residents bring their own furniture and personalise their rooms — because this is genuinely their home.

Highly Qualified Care Team

All staff are NISCC registered and complete a mandatory 9-unit annual training cycle covering Dementia awareness, Safeguarding, First Aid, Moving & Handling, and more.

Full Regulatory Transparency

RQIA registered and fully compliant. Our Governance page provides direct access to inspection reports, our complaints procedure, and all regulatory contacts.

Clear Fee Transparency

We provide a complete, up-to-date fee schedule with no hidden costs. Our admissions team walks you through every financial detail before any commitment is made.

Outstanding Location

Situated in the picturesque village of Cushendall, minutes from the marina and beaches, with the therapeutic backdrop of the Glens of Antrim landscape.

Enriching Activity Programme

From yoga and live music to pet therapy and intergenerational events, our "Life at The Glens" programme keeps residents engaged, joyful, and connected.

About Us

Over 30 Years Caring for Your Loved Ones

Located at 63 Middlepark Road in the picturesque village of Cushendall, The Glens Residential Home has been a trusted pillar of the local community for over three decades. Our thoughtfully designed single-storey bungalow layout provides a safe, accessible, and genuinely homely environment.

Under the caring leadership of Proprietor Mr. Muhammad Majeed and Registered Manager Miss Sheena Magee, we have built a proud reputation for care that is both compassionate and rigorously professional.

  • Single-storey bungalow — designed for mobility and safety
  • Experienced, dedicated leadership team with deep community ties
  • Minutes from Cushendall marina, beaches, and local scenery
